Why choose hiking?

By K. Dzumakajeva

We all know that hiking is an outdoor activity. But do actually everyone know what is hiking and how does that affect on our lives, bodies, mind and soul? Yes, I’ve mentioned all of those on purpose, because hiking actually affects not only on your muscles. Let’s start with a physical benefit.

Palm Springs, CA, USA (Credit: K. Dzumakajeva)

Boost your body

Hiking is a powerful cardio. While enjoying beautiful views you can also build strength in your glutes, quadriceps, hamstrings and the muscles in your hips and lower legs. Hiking also helps to control your weight, improves your balance and strengthens your core. Also, not that visible impact, but still useful – lowers the risk of heart disease, boosts bone density, improves blood pressure and blood sugar levels.

Improve your mental health

Bryce Canyon, Utah, USA

(Credits: K. Dzumakajeva)

Hiking is a powerful cardio. While enjoying beautiful views you can also build strength in your glutes, quadriceps, hamstrings and the muscles in your hips and lower legs. Hiking also helps to control your weight, improves your balance and strengthens your core. Also, not that visible impact, but still useful – lowers the risk of heart disease, boosts bone density, improves blood pressure and blood sugar levels.

While hiking is an outdoor activity, this cardio session can really low your stress level, boost your mood and improve your mental health. Spending quality time in the great outdoors doesn’t mean just getting visual satisfaction, it also calms anxiety and can really lead to a lower risk of depression.

Reboot yourself

Horseshoe Band, Arizona, USA

(Credits: K. Dzumakajeva)

Still didn’t get enough arguments to start hiking? Well, there is one more thing. Being closer to the nature and through this physical activity, you can actually have a conversation. You can get a lot of inspiration, motivation. Find great ideas and also get answers on your questions. It’s a very high-quality time for looking deeper into yourself and for a total reboot.

Enjoy more time with your family

Grand Canyon, AZ, USA

(Credits: K. Dzumakajeva)

And last but not the least, what can be better than spending quality time with your loved ones? Challenge yourself ang share your satisfaction. Hiking is a great way to spend more time with your friends and family and strengthen the mental connection.
